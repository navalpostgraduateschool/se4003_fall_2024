LT Bahleda

A Naval Officer, Leader, and Outdoors Enthusiast

About Me

LT Bahleda is from Waukesha, WI and enlisted in the US Navy in December 2011 as a Machinist Mate (NUC). While in the nuclear power training pipeline, he applied for an officer commissioning program and was accepted to attend the United States Naval Academy Preparatory School in 2013.

He earned his Bachelor of Science in Operations Research from the United States Naval Academy in 2018 and commissioned as a Surface Warfare Officer.

Naval Career

LT Bahleda served as the Main Propulsion Officer and Gunnery Officer onboard the USS TRUXTUN (DDG 103), from June 2018 to December 2020, completing one 5th Fleet deployment during the outbreak of the COVID virus.

He then served onboard the USS BATAAN (LHD 5), from December 2020 to November 2022, as the Auxiliary Division Officer completing an extended CNO maintenance availability period.

Personal Life

LT Bahleda is married to his wife Jaclyn and they have a young daughter. In his free time, LT Bahleda enjoys hiking and traveling. An avid outdoors enthusiast, he is always seeking new adventures in nature.
