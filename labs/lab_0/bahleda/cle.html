Welcome to the Naval Postgraduate School Collaborative Learning & Research Portal

NPS users: NPS staff, students, and faculty can login using their NPS username and password by clicking on the top button to the left to login. Please note that this is NOT your full @nps.edu email address, JUST your username.

Non-NPS users: those who login with their email address as their username, which includes external partners and guests, please click on bottom button to the left to login.

The Naval Postgraduate School Collaborative Learning Environment (CLE) is an interactive portal designed to support the learning and research activities of the Naval Postgraduate School and its strategic partners. It is built on the open source learning management system Sakai CLE. Sakai was and continues to be developed by a consortium of higher education institutions to better meet the academic and research needs of the higher education community.
